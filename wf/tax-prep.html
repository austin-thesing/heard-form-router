Depending on how you’ve structured your private practice, you could have upwards of a dozen tax forms to complete each year—not including state income tax returns.

With Heard, your tax filings are prepared by tax preparers that specialize in mental health private practices. Before tax time rolls around, we’ll also tell you how much to pay in estimated taxes so you can avoid any IRS or state penalties.

We handle all of your filings

A tax preparer familiar with therapy practices will prepare your business and personal income taxes from start to finish, including both federal and state returns.

Pay quarterly estimated taxes on time

When you join Heard, we’ll project your income and expenses, review taxes you’ve paid, and let you know how much to pay to the IRS and your state each quarter.

1-on-1 review of your returns together

Your tax expert will share your tax returns and answer any questions. Then we’ll file your return for you.

No hunting for receipts

Because Heard handles your practice’s bookkeeping, your tax preparer has everything they need to get your filings done on time.

Year-round tax guidance

We support you all year, proactively planning your taxes and looking for opportunities to save you money with deductions.

24/7 online access

​​Rest easy with access to live status updates of all your tax filings in the Heard app.
